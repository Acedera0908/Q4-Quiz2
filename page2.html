Noli Me Tangere Character List

Main Characters

Crisostomo Ibarra
  • For the people of San Diego, Crisostomo Ibarra is best known to them as being the son of Don Rafael Ibarra.

  • His great-grandfather had bought San Diego when he was still alive, so he is very well-known to the people of San Diego.

  • He studied in Europe for 7 years.
Maria Clara
  • She is the "daughter" of Kapitan Tiago.

  • She was set to marry Crisostomo Ibarra.

  • She stayed in the Beaterio de Santa Catalina for 7 years.

Padre Damaso
  • Being a Spanish priest, Padre Damaso was the parish priest in San Diego for 20 years before being replaced by Padre Salvi.

  • He was known to be a strict priest, who punished people through beating.
Padre Salvi
  • He replaced Padre Damaso, so he is the current parish priest in San Diego in the story.

  • He is described to be a thin and emaciated priest.

  • He is less stricter than Padre Damaso when it comes to punishment; he punishes people either by talking about them in his sermons or through taxing them.

Basilio
  • He is one of the two sons of Sisa; he has a younger brother named Crispin.

  • Both he and Crispin are sacristans in the parish in San Diego. They did not like being sacristans, however, as they were always abused by the Sacristan Mayor.

  • After dreaming about Crispin after leaving him in the parish, he told his mother about his plans once he and Crispin would stop being sacristans.
Crispin
  • Crispin is the younger brother of Basilio.

  • He is more timid and shy compared to Basilio.

  • He was accused of stealing money from the church, so he was punished by the Sacristan Mayor by being flogged.

  • While being punished by the Sacristan Mayor, Basilio left him in the church from fear.
